# Cookie Statement

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are often used for essential functions, preferences, or analytics.

## How this site uses cookies

**gabireze.com.br** may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- **Analytics** (e.g. Google Analytics) to measure traffic in aggregate  
- **Theme preference** (light/dark) stored in **localStorage** in your browser, where applicable  

## Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site behavior.
